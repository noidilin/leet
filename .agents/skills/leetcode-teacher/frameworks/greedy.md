# Greedy

Most algorithm problems require exhaustive enumeration to find the optimal solution. Greedy algorithms leverage information to find the optimal without exhaustive search—this is the greedy choice property. There's no fixed template; the key is careful observation to eliminate suboptimal candidates early.
