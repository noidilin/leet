# Stack and Queue

- Stacks and queues are very simple in principle—implemented with arrays or linked lists, exposing only head/tail operations, hence called "restricted data structures". While simple, their use in algorithm problems requires dedicated practice.
- Monotonic stack and monotonic queue are two variants of stacks and queues that can solve special types of problems—essential to master.
