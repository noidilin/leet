# Recursive Traversal

Deeply understanding "traversal" and "divide and conquer" as two recursive thinking modes, and placing code accurately in pre/in/post-order positions, is the key to correct recursive algorithms. "Traversal" is the prototype of DFS/backtracking; "divide and conquer" is the prototype of DP/divide-and-conquer.

## Leetcode Exercise

### Solve with Traversal Thinking

- '257. Binary Tree Paths'
- '129. Sum Root to Leaf Numbers'
- '199. Binary Tree Right Side View'
- '1022. Sum of Root To Leaf Binary Numbers'

### Solve with Divide & Conquer Thinking

- '105. Construct Binary Tree from Preorder and Inorder Traversal'
- '106. Construct Binary Tree from Inorder and Postorder Traversal'
